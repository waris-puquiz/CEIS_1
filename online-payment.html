Welcome to the electronic payment system of CENTRO ESCOLAR INTEGRATED SCHOOL- MALOLOS

Before paying, please take note of the following:

● Keep your SOA on hand to ensure correct payment details
● Fill up all the essential information.
● Choose your payment option: "ONLINE PAYMENT" at the comfort of your home or "OFFLINE PAYMENT" to the nearest designated payment outlets.
● Encode the amount to be paid.
● Provide a valid and active email address upon processing the payment.
● Bring the reference number available when you pay offline in the nearest designated payment outlets.
● Keep your receipt as proof of payment
